       Subject: Information about redistributable libraries used in QPME

The redistributable_libraries subdirectory in the QPME Kit contains copies of the licenses that 
cover the freely-available and redistributable packages used in QPME. License files are stored 
under the ./redistributable_libraries/licenses subdirectory. The respective sources are stored under 
./redistributable_libraries/sources
